Please follow these instructions to fill out the cell name and marker template.

Required Columns

These two columns are always required for every row:

  • Cell Population Name
  • Gating Definition
Other columns may be included, but they will be ignored by the validator.

1. Cell Population Name

The cell population name is the cell type from the Cell Ontology

2. Gating Definition

The gating definition is a list of comma-separated gates from the Protein Ontology or our Special Gates.
